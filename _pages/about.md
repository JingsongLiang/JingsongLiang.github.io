Jingsong Liang is a first-year PhD student in Robotics and AI at [Nanyang Technological University](https://www.ntu.edu.sg/), supervised by [Prof. Chen Lv](https://lvchen.wixsite.com/automan), [Prof. Wanhua Li](https://li-wanhua.github.io/), and [Prof. Yoonchang Sung](https://yoonchangsung.com/). He is a recipient of the [IGP Artificial Intelligence-X Scholarship](https://www.ntu.edu.sg/graduate-college/admissions/programme/interdisciplinary-graduate-programme).

He received his M.S. degree in Computer Science from the [School of Computing, National University of Singapore](https://www.comp.nus.edu.sg/), where he worked as a Research Associate with [Prof. Guillaume Sartoretti](https://www.marmotlab.org/bio.html) and [Prof. Rodney Teo](https://temasek-labs.nus.edu.sg/program/program_flightscienceswarm_tsltshr.html). He received his B.S. degree in Electrical Engineering from [Northeastern University](https://www.neu.edu.cn/).

His research interests include multimodal and embodied foundation models, deep reinforcement learning, and multi-agent systems.
