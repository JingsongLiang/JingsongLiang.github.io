I am currently a research staff at the [National University of Singapore](https://www.nus.edu.sg/), working with [Prof. Guillaume Sartoretti](https://www.marmotlab.org/bio.html) at the [Multi-Agent Robotic Motion Laboratory](https://www.marmotlab.org/) and [Prof. Rodney Teo](https://temasek-labs.nus.edu.sg/program/program_flightscienceswarm_tsltshr.html) at the [Temasek Laboratories, NUS](https://www.temasek-labs.nus.edu.sg/). Before that, I received my master's degree in computer science from National University of Singapore in 2024 and my bachelor's degree in electrical engineering from Northeastern University in 2022, while in 2021 I was a visiting student at the University of Cambridge, where I worked with [Prof. Pietro Lio](https://www.cst.cam.ac.uk/people/pl219). 

My research focuses on generative and foundation models for cooperative multi-agent learning. I am also working on multi-agent systems and deep reinforcement learning (DRL) for autonomous robotic deployments. Feel free to contact me if you'd like to discuss relevant research topics and potential collaborations.
