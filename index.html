Jingsong Jason Liang

Ph.D. Student at Nanyang Technological University

Jingsong Liang is a PhD student in Robotics and AI at Nanyang Technological University, working with Prof. Chen Lv and Prof. Wanhua Li. He is a recipient of the IGP Research Award and is affiliated with the Automated Driving and Human-Machine System Laboratory (AutoMan Research Lab @ NTU).

From 2024 to 2025, he was a Research Associate at National University of Singapore, working with Prof. Guillaume Sartoretti at the Multi-Agent Robotic Motion Laboratory (MARMot Lab @ NUS) and Prof. Rodney Teo at Temasek Laboratories. He received his M.S. degree in Computer Science from the School of Computing, National University of Singapore in 2024, and his B.S. degree in Electrical Engineering from the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Northeastern University in 2022.

His research interests include multimodal large language models, autonomous exploration/navigation, and multi-agent systems.

Community Service: Reviewer or PC Member for IEEE RA-L, ICRA, IROS, ICLR, and AAAI.

Happy to chat more about relevant research and collaborations!
